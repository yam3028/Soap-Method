About the SOAP Method

The SOAP method is a simple but effective approach to Bible study that helps you dig deeper into Scripture and apply it to your life.

S - Scripture

Write out the Bible passage word for word. This helps you slow down and absorb what you're reading.

O - Observation

What do you notice about the passage? Who is speaking? Who is the audience? What are the key words or phrases? What stands out to you?

A - Application

How does this passage apply to your life right now? What is God saying to you through this passage? How can you live out these truths today?

P - Prayer

Write a prayer based on what you've learned. Ask God to help you apply this truth in your life.

The SOAP method was popularized by Pastor Wayne Cordeiro and has been adopted by many Christians as an effective way to engage with Scripture.
